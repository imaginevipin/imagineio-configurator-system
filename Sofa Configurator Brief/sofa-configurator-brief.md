# Sofa Configurator

## Overview

This is a modular sofa configurator built using a component-based system. The product should not be treated as a fixed layout but as a flexible assembly of modules.

---

## Default Configuration

* 1 Corner Unit
* 2 Armless Seat Units
* 1 Ottoman Unit

---

## Module Inventory

### Seating Units

* Armless Seat
* End Seat (Left Arm, Right Arm)
* Corner Unit

### Extensions

* Ottoman Unit

---

## Module Rules

* Armless Seat connects on both sides
* End Seat connects on only one side
* Corner Unit connects in perpendicular directions
* Ottoman can attach to any open edge or remain standalone

---

## Constraints

* Minimum 2 modules required
* No floating modules
* Corner cannot connect directly to another corner
* Ottoman cannot be placed between two seats

---

## Build Controls

* Add Module
* Remove Module
* Reorder Modules
* Rotate Module
